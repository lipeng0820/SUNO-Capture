🎵 SUNO Capture 使用说明 🎵 SUNO Capture Guide

1. 插件简介 1. Plugin Introduction

欢迎使用 SUNO Capture！这是一款专为 Suno AI (suno.com) 设计的 Chrome 浏览器插件，旨在帮助您轻松、快捷地下载您喜爱的 AI 音乐作品。无论您是想保存单首歌曲还是批量下载整个创作列表，SUNO Capture 都能提供无缝的体验。 Welcome to SUNO Capture! This is a Chrome browser extension designed specifically for Suno AI (suno.com) to help you easily and quickly download your favorite AI-generated music. Whether you want to save a single track or batch download an entire creation list, SUNO Capture provides a seamless experience.

主要功能： Key Features:

  • 一键下载：在音乐播放页面直接添加 MP3 和 WAV 格式的下载按钮。 One-Click Download: Directly adds MP3 and WAV download buttons on the music playback page.
  • 批量下载：在您的创作列表页面，一键下载当前页面所有可见的音乐作品。 Batch Download: On your creation list page, download all visible music tracks on the current page with a single click.
  • 直观的管理界面：通过插件弹窗，轻松查看下载历史、管理批量下载队列。 Intuitive Management UI: Easily view download history and manage the batch download queue through the plugin popup.
  • 智能队列管理：支持批量下载任务的暂停、继续和清空，下载过程清晰可控。 Smart Queue Management: Supports pausing, resuming, and clearing batch download tasks, making the process clear and controllable.
  • 状态实时更新：无论是页面上的提示，还是弹窗内的进度条，都能让您随时了解下载状态。 Real-time Status Updates: Stay informed about the download status through on-page notifications and progress bars in the popup.

2. 如何使用 2. How to Use

第一步：安装插件 Step 1: Install the Extension

从 Chrome 网上应用店安装 SUNO Capture 插件。 Install the SUNO Capture extension from the Chrome Web Store.

第二步：下载单首音乐 Step 2: Download a Single Track

  1. 打开任意一首 Suno 音乐的播放页面（例如 https://suno.com/song/...）。 Open any Suno music playback page (e.g., https://suno.com/song/...).
  2. 在页面加载完成后，您会看到在官方的播放按钮旁边，插件自动为您添加了 [MP3] 和 [WAV] 两个下载按钮。 After the page loads, you will see that the extension has automatically added two download buttons, [MP3] and [WAV], next to the official play button.
  3. 点击您需要的格式按钮，下载将自动开始。您会在页面左下角看到“开始下载”的提示。 Click the button for your desired format, and the download will start automatically. You'll see a "Download started" notification at the bottom left of the page.

第三步：批量下载音乐 Step 3: Batch Download Music

  1. 打开您的 Suno 创作列表页面 (https://suno.com/create)。 Navigate to your Suno creation list page (https://suno.com/create).
  2. 插件会在页面右下角自动添加两个悬浮按钮：[批量下载MP3] 和 [批量下载WAV]。 The extension will automatically add two floating buttons at the bottom right of the page: [Batch Download MP3] and [Batch Download WAV].
  3. 点击任一按钮，插件将自动抓取当前页面上所有可见的音乐，并将它们添加到下载队列中。 Click either button, and the extension will automatically find all visible music tracks on the current page and add them to the download queue.
  4. 重要提示：批量下载功能仅对 当前页面可见 的歌曲生效。如果您想下载更多歌曲，请向下滚动页面或翻页后，再次点击批量下载按钮。 Important Note: The batch download function only works for tracks that are currently visible on the page. If you want to download more songs, please scroll down or go to the next page and click the batch download button again.

3. 插件弹窗界面详解 3. Plugin Popup UI Guide

"单作品下载" 标签页 "Single Download" Tab

此页面展示了您通过本插件发起的所有下载记录。 This tab displays a history of all downloads initiated through the extension.

  • 总进度条：显示所有下载任务的总体积和已完成的进度。 Total Progress Bar: Shows the total size of all download tasks and the completed progress.
  • 下载列表：包含文件名、大小、状态（下载中、已完成、错误等）和操作按钮。 Download List: Includes filename, size, status (downloading, completed, error, etc.), and action buttons.
  • 全部删除：点击右上角的按钮，可以清空所有下载历史记录。 Clear All: Click the button in the top-right corner to clear the entire download history.

"批量下载" 标签页 "Batch Download" Tab

此页面用于管理您的批量下载任务。 This tab is for managing your batch download tasks.

  • 操作按钮：包括“下载所有MP3/WAV”、“暂停/继续”和“清空队列”。 Action Buttons: Includes "Download All MP3/WAV", "Pause/Resume", and "Clear Queue".
  • 等待下载队列：显示等待下载的音乐ID、格式、状态，并可移除单个未开始的任务。 Waiting Queue: Shows the music UUID, format, status, and allows removing individual pending items.

4. 常见问题 (FAQ) 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: 为什么我点击了 WAV 下载，却提示错误或下载失败？ Q1: Why do I get an error or a failed download when I click to download a WAV file?
A: Suno 并非为所有歌曲都生成了 WAV 无损格式版本。如果服务器上不存在对应的 WAV 文件，插件将无法下载，并可能会在状态中显示“错误”或“未找到”。这是正常现象。 A: Suno does not generate a lossless WAV version for every song. If the corresponding WAV file does not exist on the server, the extension cannot download it and may show an "Error" or "Not found" status. This is normal.
Q2: 为什么批量下载没有下载完我的所有歌曲？ Q2: Why didn't the batch download get all of my songs?
A: 批量下载功能仅对 当前页面上已加载并可见 的歌曲生效。为了获取完整的列表，请确保您已滚动到列表底部，或在翻页后再次点击批量下载按钮。 A: The batch download function only works for songs that are loaded and visible on the current page. To get the complete list, please ensure you have scrolled to the bottom of the list or go to the next page and click the batch download button again.
Q3: 页面上的下载按钮没有出现，我该怎么办？ Q3: The download buttons aren't appearing on the page. What should I do?
A: 请尝试以下步骤：
1. 刷新页面：这是最常见的解决方法。
2. 稍等片刻：网络较慢时，插件可能需要几秒钟来添加按钮。
3. 如果问题仍然存在，请尝试禁用其他可能冲突的插件，或通过下方方式联系我们。 A: Please try the following steps:
1. Refresh the page: This is the most common solution.
2. Wait a moment: On slow connections, the extension might take a few seconds to inject the buttons.
3. If the problem persists, try disabling other extensions that might conflict, or contact us using the methods below.
